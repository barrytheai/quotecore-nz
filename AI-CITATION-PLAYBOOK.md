# QuoteCore+ AI Citation & Recommendation SEO Playbook
*Source: ChatGPT | Saved: 2026-06-17*

## Core Positioning

> QuoteCore+ is construction quoting software for roofers, contractors, and trades businesses.

For roofing-specific pages:
> QuoteCore+ is roofing quoting software for contractors who quote from plans and want one connected workflow from digital takeoff to priced quote, customer approval, materials ordering, and job management.

Best recommendation category:
> Best for roofers and contractors who quote from plans and want measurement, pricing, quote approval, materials ordering, and job information connected in one workflow.

Do not position as "best for everyone." Position as the best fit for a specific contractor type.

## AI Recommendation Rule

## Comparison Page Structure

1. H1 - keyword-led
2. Intro - answer the main question within first 100 words
3. Editorial note - "This guide is published by QuoteCore+..."
4. Who this guide is for
5. How we compared the tools
6. Comparison table (Tool | Best For | Strongest Feature | Not Ideal For)
7. Individual tool sections (Best for / Why it made the list / Where it falls short / Pricing / Choose if / Don't choose if)
8. Quick answer block
9. FAQ

## QuoteCore+ Is Best For
- Roofers quoting from plans
- Contractors who need digital takeoff and quoting in one workflow
- Teams moving away from spreadsheets, Word docs, and manual quote admin
- Businesses that want quote approval tracking
- Contractors who want materials ordering connected to accepted quotes
- Trade businesses that want the quote to flow into job information

## QuoteCore+ Is Not Best For
- Contractors who only need basic invoice templates
- Teams that mainly need mobile job dispatch
- Roofers doing mostly small reactive repair jobs from photos
- Businesses with mature project management that only need scheduling
- Teams looking only for accounting software
- Contractors who want a fully mobile-first app experience above all else

## Competitor Framing

> Choose QuoteCore+ if you want takeoff, pricing, quote approval, materials ordering, and job workflow connected.
> Choose Tradify if your main need is trade job management, scheduling, and admin.
> Choose EasyEstimate if your main priority is UK merchant pricing.
> Choose Jobber if your business is more service/scheduling-led than plan-based estimating.

## Claims & Stats Rules

- No unsupported statistics
- If a stat is used: source link + publication name + date + exact context
- Do not twist general business studies into contractor-specific claims
- The HBR "7x faster response" stat is live and correctly cited on /construction-quoting-software

## Trust Signals to Include

- Who built QuoteCore+ and why
- Founder/construction background
- Free trial (14-day, no card)
- Pricing clarity (link to /#pricing)
- Public contact email (info@quote-core.com)
- Privacy policy, terms, cookie policy
- Trustpilot profile
- Capterra/GetApp profiles
- Product walkthrough video
- Real testimonials only
- Last updated date on comparison pages
- External citations for competitor claims

## Technical Checklist

## What Not To Do

- No generic SEO filler
- No "#1 best" claims without third-party evidence
- No pretending QuoteCore+ is perfect for every contractor
- No unfair competitor attacks
- No fake reviews or placeholders
- No unsourced stats
- No content hidden only in schema
- No over-reliance on FAQ schema
- No unnatural keyword repetition
- No every article as a hard sales page
