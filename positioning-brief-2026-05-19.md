# QuoteCore+ Positioning Direction — May 2026

## Core shift
From: Roofing quoting software for roofers
To: Measuring and quoting software for construction businesses

Roofing is our credibility. Construction is the market.

## New positioning statement
QuoteCore+ helps construction businesses measure, price, quote, and manage jobs faster — from first measurement to client-ready quote to accepted job.

## Target market (expanded)
Any construction trade that measures and quotes regularly:
- Roofing (origin story, still core)
- Cladding, flooring, fencing, landscaping, decking
- Sheds/outbuildings, general building, exterior works, renovation trades
- Any trade measuring areas, lengths, components, materials, labour, margins

Focus: businesses still on spreadsheets, paper notes, manual takeoffs, Word docs, emails, disconnected admin.

## Strongest messaging angles
- "If your job starts with measurements and ends with a quote, QuoteCore+ can probably save you hours."
- Built from roofing. Useful across construction.
- Measure it. Price it. Quote it. Manage it.
- Stop rebuilding quotes from scratch.
- For construction teams still quoting from spreadsheets, notes, and memory.
- The faster way to turn measurements into professional quotes.
- Quoting software for trades where measurements actually matter.
- From plan to quote without the spreadsheet mess.
- Built by someone who spent years quoting construction jobs the hard way.

## Tone direction
Practical, direct, trade-friendly. Plain language.

USE:
- "Quotes take too long."
- "Spreadsheets break."
- "Measurements get copied too many times."
- "Jobs get messy when the details are spread everywhere."
- "QuoteCore+ keeps the measuring, pricing, quoting, and job info in one place."

AVOID:
- "Revolutionising the construction ecosystem"
- "End-to-end digital transformation"
- "AI-powered operational excellence"
- "Next-generation workflow optimisation"

## Updated founder story
Shaun spent years in roofing and construction, dealing with the same quoting mess most trades still deal with: measuring jobs, copying numbers into spreadsheets, building quotes manually, ordering materials, tracking approvals, and keeping job details organised. QuoteCore+ started with roofing because that was the problem Shaun knew best, but the system now solves a wider construction problem.

Use Shaun's roofing background as proof. Make the conclusion broader.

## What stays the same
- Built by someone who lived the problem
- Practical, simple, construction-first software
- Faster quoting, less admin, professional output
- Measurements entered once, pricing logic set up once
- Fewer errors, better organisation, faster response times
- Quote to job workflow in one place

## What changes
- Stop making every message sound like it only applies to roofing
- Roofing examples = examples, not the whole market
- Broader language: measurements, materials, labour, components, margins, plans, takeoffs, quotes, approvals, job details, orders
